BlaskSwan Choreography

Started in 2024 by Ayush Srivastava, BlackSwan choreography is a Dance company. The main motto of the company is to make everyone believe that dance is for everyone. Irrespective of age or experience, we believe that dance should be a happy escape for everyone. This company aims to provide a safe space for everyone to learn, experience and grow the uniqueness of each individual and to cherish the joy of dancing.
